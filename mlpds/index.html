FAQ and Info

  • Why do I get an error when posting links, images, or want I try to use voice activity in chat? — A mod will have to give you the voice role. Ping @mod to request the role.
  • Why do we have two general chats and what's the difference? — There's no difference except for the names. Two general chats allows for multiple conversations when the server gets busy.
  • What is considered safe, suggestive or explicit? — Here are our current guidelines:
    • Explicit material typically includes gore, genitals, or other "extreme" material. Keep this to the adult channels.
    • Suggestive material can include art with emphasis on genital areas, suggestive situations, or other borderline material. It's a fuzzy definition, so tag and break links if you aren't sure or ask a mod.
    • Safe material is generally whatever isn't included in the previous two categories.
  • How do I get access to the adult channels? — If you're over 18, use !roles nsfw on to opt-in or !roles nsfw off to opt-out.
  • How can I link something suggestive without having discord show a preview image? — To break suggestive links so they're not automatically previewed in others' clients, surround the link with angle brackets like so: <http://example.com/image.png>.
  • What's a "drawalong"? — Drawalongs are a group drawing exercise where a small group of artists will all draw from the same prompt for 30 minutes. The point of the time limit is to try to get as much done on the piece as possible in a short amount of time. After the first 30 minutes, there's a short break for critique among the participants (don't forget rule #2!). Finally there is 30 more minutes for everyone to finish their pieces using the critiques.
  • OMG stop with the rules and FAQ and blah blah blah already, how do I get in so I can talk and see the channels?! — Well if you've made it this far, you're ready to know how to get started. Send a private message to @princess luna with the command !warden access mlpds and you'll get access to the rest of the rooms and the ability to send messages.
  • Pegesi? Earth ponies? What are you, some sort of small, colorful horses? — Silly group names are silly! If you want to join one of the pony groups, you can manage your group affiliation by using the !roles pony clear/earth/pegasus/unicorn/equestrian command.
  • I've got a different question. Who can I ask? — Feel free to ping a moderator by using @mod in your message.
